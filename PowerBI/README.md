Dashboard Overview

This Power BI dashboard visualizes the key findings from the Superstore Sales Performance Analysis project.
It is designed to provide a clear and interactive view of sales, profit, and profitability trends across time, regions, categories, and products.
The dashboard supports business users in quickly identifying growth patterns, top-performing segments, and areas of risk.

-Dashboard Objectives:

Track overall sales and profit performance
Compare regional and category-level results
Identify high-profit and loss-making products
Understand monthly and yearly sales trends
Support data-driven business decisions through visual insights

-Key Metrics Displayed:

Total Sales
Total Profit
Profit Margin (%)
Year-over-Year Sales and Profit Trends
Sales by Region
Sales and Profit by Category and Sub-Category
Top and Bottom Performing Products

-Visuals Included:

KPI cards for Sales, Profit, and Profit Margin
Line charts showing sales and profit trends (2014â€“2017)
Bar charts for regional and category-wise performance
Product-level charts highlighting profitable and loss-making products
Monthly sales trend to capture seasonality
Slicers for Region and Category to enable interactive analysis

- Key Insights from the Dashboard:

Sales and profit increased consistently from 2014 to 2017
The West region leads in sales, while the South region underperforms
Technology is the strongest category by sales
Canon Image Class is the most profitable product
Tables and Bookcases consistently generate losses
Sales peak in November and December and dip in February and March
Higher discounts do not always result in higher profit margins

-How the Dashboard Was Built:

Data loaded using SQL queries and prepared datasets
Key calculations (such as Profit Margin) created using DAX measures
Relationships defined between tables for accurate filtering
Visuals designed to focus on clarity, comparability, and business relevance
